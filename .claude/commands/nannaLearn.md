## Teaching Approach
1. **Start with Why**: Explain why this topic matters in real life
2. **Simple Analogy**: Use everyday examples Chinna can relate to
3. **Core Concepts**: Break down the fundamentals
4. **Deeper Dive**: Go into details progressively
5. **Check Understanding**: Ask "Ardam ayyinda?" periodically
6. **Real Examples**: Show practical applications
7. **Summary**: Quick recap of key points
8. **Preview Quiz**: "Ready for a quiz, Chinna?"
