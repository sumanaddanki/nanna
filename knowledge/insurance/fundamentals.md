# Insurance Fundamentals

## What is Insurance?

Risk transfer mechanism where many people pool resources to protect against uncertain losses.

### Core Principle
**Law of Large Numbers**: Predict aggregate losses for a group even when individual losses are unpredictable.

---

## Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Premium** | Regular payment for coverage |
| **Policy** | Contract between insurer and insured |
| **Coverage/Sum Assured** | Maximum payout amount |
| **Deductible** | Amount you pay before insurance kicks in |
| **Copay** | Fixed amount you pay per service |
| **Claim** | Request for payment under policy |
| **Exclusion** | What's NOT covered |
| **Rider** | Additional coverage add-on |
| **Underwriting** | Risk assessment process |
| **Actuary** | Risk and premium calculator |

---

## Types of Insurance

### Life Insurance
Pays beneficiaries upon death of insured.

**Types:**
- **Term Life**: Coverage for specific period
- **Whole Life**: Lifetime coverage + savings
- **ULIP**: Unit Linked Insurance Plan (Insurance + Investment)

### Health Insurance
Covers medical expenses.

**Key Concepts:**
- Network hospitals
- Cashless claims
- Pre-existing conditions
- Waiting period

### Property Insurance
Covers damage to property (home, auto).

### Liability Insurance
Covers legal obligations to others.

## The Insurance Process

```
1. Application → 2. Underwriting → 3. Policy Issued → 4. Premium Payment → 5. Claim (if event occurs) → 6. Settlement
```

---

## Underwriting

### What is Underwriting?
Evaluating risk to determine:
1. Whether to insure
2. Premium amount
3. Terms and conditions

### Factors Considered
- Age, health history
- Occupation
- Lifestyle (smoking, etc.)
- Coverage amount requested

## InsurTech Trends

### 1. AI/ML in Underwriting
- Automated risk assessment
- Faster approvals
- Personalized pricing

### 2. Telematics
- Usage-based insurance (UBI)
- Pay-how-you-drive auto insurance

### 3. Parametric Insurance
- Automatic payout when trigger occurs
- Example: Payout if rainfall < X mm

### 4. Embedded Insurance
- Insurance integrated at point of sale
- Buy phone → Insurance offered instantly

### 5. Blockchain
- Smart contracts for claims
- Fraud prevention

---

## Claims Process

### Steps
1. **Intimation**: Notify insurer of event
2. **Documentation**: Submit required documents
3. **Assessment**: Insurer evaluates claim
4. **Settlement**: Payment or rejection

### Types of Settlement
- **Cashless**: Direct payment to provider
- **Reimbursement**: Pay first, get refund
